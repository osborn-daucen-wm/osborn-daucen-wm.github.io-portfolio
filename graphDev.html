My Graphic Design Skills

Photoshop

I am proficient with Adobe Photoshop and have been using it for five years and professionally for three years. I use this type of graphic design program to edit photos, create custom raster images, and to setup documents for professional printing or web development.

Illustrator

I have the same amount of experience in Illustrator as I do Photoshop. However, I prefer Illustrator more for the cleaner design aspect and use of Vector designing. I use this program to setup laser cuts, make custom designs, make stickers, logos, and web development images.

InDesign

InDesign is good for creating larger files for printing and publishing in a more organized way. I have used this program for making Menu's, a Magazine, a portfolio, and a tutorial.




Blender

Blender is a 3D modeling software that I have been using for seven years. I have created 3D models, animations, and concepts with this software.

Video Editing

I am proficient in three video editing softwares from Adobe. These iclude Adobe's Premier, After Effects, and Animate. I use this to create tutorials, promotional videos, animations, and even special effects. I have been a part of a film festival and my team got first place during this festival for the video we produced.
