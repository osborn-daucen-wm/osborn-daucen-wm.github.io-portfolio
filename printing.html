My Printing and Manufacturing Capabilities

3D Printing

I have been 3D printing for years. I have my own printer at home and have professionally worked for two labs that hold 3D printers. I have worked at West-MEC's create-lab which held five Makerbots that I maintained and two Dremel 3D40's. I have also worked at Arizona State Universitie's FSE 3D Print Lab for the Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering where I ran three Makerbots, an Ultimaker 2, four Dremel 3D45's, a Makerbot Z18, and the expensive Stratasys Dimension SST 1200es and Mojo.

Laser Engraving

Again, I have professionally worked at West-MEC and ASU where they both tasked me with running and maintaining Laser Cutters. I have worked with the Rayjet at West-MEC and at ASU there are four different Universal Laser Systems I was in charge of. I have been tasked with engraving and cutting all sorts of materials including acrylic, metal, leather, bottles, glass panels, and wood.

Vinyl Printing/Cutting

I have used Roland's BN-20 to cut and print Vinyl for posters and vinyl meant for walls. I have used this material to make banners, large format prints, and even t-shirts.




UV Printing

I have worked on Roland's LEF-12 UV printer for making awards, specialty item, cards, announcements, and even golf balls! I have become proficient in printing both white and metallic ink as well as CMYK inks.
